Grandma Olga's Sauce

  • Time: All Day
  • Serves: The Whole Family
Ingredients Quantity
Crushed Tomatoes 1 lg can
Tomato Sauce 1 lg can
Ground Beef 2 lbs
Parsley
Garlic
Red Pepper Flakes
Mollasses 1/2 cup
Corn Syrup 1/4 cup
Canned Mushrooms 2 sm cans
Curry Powder

Step 1: Heat everything to a bubble then reduce the heat.

Step 2: Stir occasionally. Don't burn the sauce!

Step 3: Remove grease.

Classic Green Bean Casserole

  • Time: Half Hour
  • Serves: A Few People
Ingredients Quantity
Campbell's Cream of Mushroom Soup 1 can
Milk 1/2 cup
Ground Black Pepper 1/8 tsp
French Green Beans 2 cans
French's® Original Crispy Fried Onions 1 pkg
Soy Sauce 1 tsp
Shredded Chicken
Better Than Bullion 1 tsp

Step 1: Mix ingrediants, reserving half the onions.

Step 2: Bake at 350°F until hot.

Step 3: Stir, top with remaining onions, and bake for five more minutes.
